Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about buying property in Patna

How to buy property in Patna?

Identify projects with 100% Genuine Land, check legal documents, and ensure transparent payment schedules. Our team guides you step by step through the entire process, from site selection to final registry.

What is Genuine Land and the registry process?

Genuine Land means clear title, verified documents, and transparent processes. Registry is the legal process that transfers ownership of plots to buyers, making you the lawful owner of the property.

Benefits of investing in plotted townships?

Plots offer higher appreciation potential, flexibility in construction timing and design, better long-term ROI compared to flats, and the freedom to build according to your specific needs and preferences.

What payment options are available?

We offer flexible installment plans, attractive spot booking discounts, and special investor packages. All legal documentation and deeds are handled with complete transparency by our expert team.
